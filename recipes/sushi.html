Sushi Recipe

Believe it or not, you don't have to be an expert to make amazing sushi!

This sushi recipe is simple and delicious. It is a fun recipe, and after making it a few times, you will be a sushi pro!

Ingredients

For the Sushi Rice:

  • 1 cup sushi rice (short-grain rice)
  • 1 1/4 cups water
  • 2 tbsp rice vinegar
  • 1 tbsp sugar
  • 1/2 tsp salt

For the Rolls:

  • 4 sheets of nori (seaweed)
  • 1 cucumber, julienned
  • 1 avocado, sliced
  • 1/2 lb (225g) imitation crab sticks or fresh crab meat
  • Soy sauce, for dipping
  • Pickled ginger (optional)
  • Wasabi (optional)

Steps

  1. Prepare the sushi rice:

    1. Rinse the sushi rice under cold water until the water runs clear.
    2. Combine the rice and water in a rice cooker and cook according to the rice cooker's instructions. Alternatively, bring the water and rice to a boil in a pot, reduce the heat to low, cover, and simmer for 18-20 minutes.
    3. emove from heat and let it sit covered for 10 minutes. In a small saucepan, heat the rice vinegar, sugar, and salt over low heat until the sugar and salt dissolve. Do not let it boil.
    4. Transfer the cooked rice to a large bowl and gently fold in the vinegar mixture. Allow the rice to cool to room temperature.

  2. Assemble the sushi rolls:

    1. Place a bamboo sushi mat on a flat surface and cover it with plastic wrap. Place a sheet of nori, shiny side down, on the mat.
    2. Wet your hands with water to prevent the rice from sticking. Take a handful of sushi rice and spread it evenly over the nori, leaving about 1 inch (2.5 cm) at the top edge uncovered.
    3. Arrange a few slices of cucumber, avocado, and crab sticks horizontally across the middle of the rice.
    4. Using the bamboo mat, carefully roll the sushi away from you, applying gentle pressure to shape the roll. Seal the edge of the nori with a little water.

  3. Slice the sushi rolls:

    1. Using a sharp knife, slice the sushi roll into 6-8 pieces. Wipe the knife with a damp cloth between cuts to ensure clean slices.

  4. Serve:

    1. Arrange the sushi pieces on a plate and serve with soy sauce, pickled ginger, and wasabi on the side.
