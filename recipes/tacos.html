Taco Recipe

Looking for a delicious, quick, and affordable meal the whole family will love?! Look no further!

In this recipe, we will show you our favorite taco recipe of all time. It is the perfect meal for a busy family looking for some quick and easy to make during the weekday. Who doesn't love tacos?!

Ingredients

For the Beef:

  • 1 lb (450g) ground beef
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tsp ground cumin
  • 1 tsp chili powder
  • 1/2 tsp paprika
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/4 tsp black pepper
  • 1/4 cup tomato sauce
  • 1/4 cup water

For the Tacos:

  • Corn tortillas (about 8)
  • Fresh cilantro, chopped
  • 1 lime, cut into wedges
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped
  • Salsa (optional)
  • Crumbled queso fresco or grated cheese (optional)

Steps

  1. Prepare the beef filling:

    Heat a large skillet over medium-high heat. Add the ground beef and cook, breaking it up with a spoon, until it begins to brown. Add the chopped onion and minced garlic to the skillet. Cook until the onion is soft and translucent, about 5 minutes. Stir in the cumin, chili powder, paprika, salt, and black pepper. Cook for another 2 minutes, allowing the spices to bloom. Add the tomato sauce and water to the skillet. Reduce the heat to low and let the mixture simmer for about 10 minutes, or until the sauce has thickened and the beef is fully cooked. Stir occasionally.

  2. Heat the tortillas:

    In a separate dry skillet or directly over a gas flame, heat the corn tortillas for about 30 seconds on each side, or until they are warm and slightly charred. Keep them warm in a clean towel.

  3. Assemble the tacos:

    Spoon some of the beef mixture onto each warm tortilla. Top with chopped cilantro and finely chopped onion. Add a squeeze of fresh lime juice to each taco. If desired, top with salsa and crumbled queso fresco or grated cheese.

  4. Serve immediately:

    Serve the tacos hot, accompanied by additional lime wedges and your favorite salsa. Enjoy your delicious, simple, and authentic tacos!
